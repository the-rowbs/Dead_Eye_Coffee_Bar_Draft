The Dead Eye Experience

Dead Eye Coffee Bar is built on the simple idea of introducing a unique excitement from every cup of coffee that leaves our hands to yours. Our passion is not just coffee, but to bring out the uniqueness of each bean to stimulate your senses in each sip.

The true enjoyment of coffee is the experience each cup brings. The aroma of each inhale, the layers in each sip, the conversation induced by the complex flavors, are all part of the coffee experience. This is the environment we create every day at the shop and the reason we work tirelessly to collaborate with our community. We integrate the knowledge of our local business partners to enrich each cup of coffee we serve. We are dedicated to bringing the coffee experience to the community.
